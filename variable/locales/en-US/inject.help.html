Details

The timestamp is the current time in milliseconds since January 1st, 1970 (unix timestamp)

The node can be triggered manually by clicking on it or by configuring an interval.

To output every 15 minutes, use the Interval option.

The maximum interval can be 596 hours or 24 days.

Note

Interval between times and Time use the standard cron system
